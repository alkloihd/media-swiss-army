Media Swiss Army - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-04

Media Swiss Army does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share your data. We do not have a server.

What the App accesses

  • Your Photos library, after you grant permission, so you can pick photos and videos to clean, compress, or stitch. We use Apple's standard PhotosPicker, which only gives the app the items you choose.

What the App does with that access

  • Reads metadata from the items you picked.
  • Writes cleaned or processed copies back to your Photos library when you choose to save them.
  • Optionally deletes originals, recoverable from Recently Deleted for 30 days, only when you explicitly enable that option.

What the App does not do

  • No analytics. No tracking. No ad networks.
  • No accounts. No login.
  • No cloud processing.
  • No data sold or shared with third parties.

Data linked to you

The photos and videos you process are part of your library. They are processed on your device and are never transmitted to us. Per Apple's privacy definitions, data processed only on-device is not collected.

Changes to this policy

If anything here changes, the updated date at the top will be edited and the change will be announced in the app's release notes.
